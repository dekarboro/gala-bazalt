WHAT IS BASALT ROVING

ECOLOGICAL MATERIAL OF THE FUTURE

ECOLOGICAL MATERIAL OF THE FUTURE

Basalt fibers are naturally occurring, continuous fibers derived from melting volcanic basalt rocks.

This environmentally f riendly material, often called the 'green material of the 21st century', is completely recyclable, and produces no emissions or production waste. It has been conclusively demonstrated that basalt fibers are neither toxic nor carcinogenic.

The evolution of modern technology necessitates the development of modern, strong, dependable, long-lasting, and cost-effective composite materials . As a material of the new generation, basalt fibers offer immense potential.

Our proposal entails the production of the most technologically advanced, economically viable, and ecologically material, which offers a broad spectrum of uses and the potential to create new, state-of-the-art products.
