NEW GENERATION MATERIAL

NEW GENERATION MATERIAL

Products constructed from basalt threads are high-quality and cost-effective alternatives to metal and metal-plastic items across various industries. Moreover, basalt thread possesses unique and irreplaceable properties. Its notably valuable features include strength, resistance to corrosion and chemical effects, the ability to withstand heavy loads, lightness, non-flammability, as well as thermal and acoustic insulation. It is also environmentally friendly, along with several other attributes.
