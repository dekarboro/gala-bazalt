PROJECT HISTORY

PROJECT HISTORY

Our specialists are the innovators behind the invention and development of basalt fiber product technology. This technology, initially discovered and developed in Ukraine in the early 1960s, saw its first plant built on the outskirts of Kyiv in the mid-1980s. Ukrainian scientists and specialists have been tirelessly enhancing production processes ever since. With the aid of these Ukrainian experts, nearly all factories across Europe and Asia have been established. This unique experience, along with the latest advancements, forms the foundation of our new project.

This project entails the establishment of a technological laboratory to analyze product quality. It also aims to further refine the technological process and develop new basalt products to meet customer needs. This, in turn, expands the market reach of our products.

Our team consists of specialists with extensive theoretical knowledge and extensive practical experience: world-famous scientists, designers, technologists, technology engineers, authors of scientific and technical publications, holders of numerous patents, specialists in the field of basalt fiber production technology and composite materials based on them, as well as specialists in production management. All members of our team have extensive experience and come from the scientific circles of Ukraine.

The portfolio of completed projects over the past 10 years includes:

  • ▶Construction of three mineral wool production plants;
  • ▶Modernization and installation of lines for processing glass and basalt fiber into laminate and pressed boards;
  • ▶Design and implementation of the construction of four plants for the production of basalt roving and products from it.
